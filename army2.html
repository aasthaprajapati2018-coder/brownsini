The history of the Indian Army is very old and proud. It started during the time of the East India Company in the 1600s, when the British formed Indian soldiers to protect their trade.

After India became independent in 1947, the Indian Army became the national army of India. Since then, it has fought several important wars to protect the country, including wars in 1947-48, 1962, 1965, 1971, and 1999.

The Indian Army has a rich heritage of bravery and sacrifice, with many soldiers receiving prestigious awards for their courage in battle.

The motto of the Indian Army is "Service Before Self," reflecting their dedication to the nation above all else.
